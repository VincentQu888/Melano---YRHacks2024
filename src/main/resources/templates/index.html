Nevus

Nevus is a non-cancer growth on the skin formed by a cluster of cells called melanocytes. Usually dark and raised from the skin, it is also known as a mole.

Melanoma

Melanoma is a type of skin cancer which starts in the melanocytes. Specifically, melanoma typically starts on skin that's often exposed to the sun. This includes the skin on the arms, back, face and legs.

Diagnosis is the first step to treatment.

Melano takes an image of a skin lesion and uses an AI model trained on thousands of images to determine whether or not the person is at risk of melanoma. Melanoma is the 3rd most common type of skin cancer and 17th most common cancer worldwide. Ensuring early awareness of the condition is vital, as melanoma is the most invasive type of skin cancer, and can become fatal in as low as 6 weeks. However, if treated early, melanoma has a very high survival rate.

About Melano.

Melano is a project made for YRHacks2024, with an end goal of aiding in the fight against cancer. Not only as a preliminary tool to assist medical professionals in diagnosing patients, but also as a convenience to the potentially affected. Melano's goal is to make it easier and more reasonable for people to become aware of a potentially life-changing condition.
